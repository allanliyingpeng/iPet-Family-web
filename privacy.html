Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

Welcome to iPet Family ("we", "our", or "the App"). We value your privacy and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Information Collection

To provide our services, we may collect the following information:

  • Camera Access: Used to take pet photos for breed identification and AI art generation. We will not access your camera without your consent.
  • Photo Library Access: Used to select existing pet photos for analysis.
  • Device Information: Including device model, OS version, etc., used to optimize app performance.
  • Usage Data: Including feature usage frequency, crash logs, etc., used to improve our services.

2. Information Use

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide pet breed identification services
  • Provide AI pet health consultation services
  • Generate pet art photos
  • Improve and optimize app features
  • Provide customer support

3. Information Storage & Protection

We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your personal information:

  • Photos you upload are only used for immediate analysis and are not permanently stored on our servers
  • Chat history is stored only on your local device
  • We use encryption to protect data transmission

4. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AI (Gemini): For AI analysis and generation features
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

5. Advertising

This app contains advertisements. We use Google AdMob to display ads, which may collect device identifiers for personalized advertising. You can limit ad tracking in your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Withdraw your consent

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant changes through in-app notifications.
